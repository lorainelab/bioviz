Credits

Companies that contributed tooling that makes IGB more accessible to users and developers

The IGB team gratefully acknowledges the intrepid EJ Technologies, which creates install4J, one of the best products (in our opinion) currently available to support native installers for Java-based projects. Starting with IGB 8.3, the IGB project uses install4J to distribute IGB to users.

The IGB team also acknowledges the mighty, broad-shouldered Atlassian, which granted us a free license to use their Jira and Confluence products. Jira and Confluence are a core part of our development and documentation processes. Thank you, thank you, thank you!

If you are considering purchasing these products, the IGB team would be happy to give you our honest (glowing) opinion. Feel free to get in touch - contact Ann at aloraine@uncc.edu.

Please support the companies that make tools that support open source software in genomics!
