BioViz Connect is a new way to view and interact with data files in your CyVerse account. Now you can view your data files in IGB with a click of button. You can also add IGB-specific meta-data to files, such as genome version and track color, and make notes on your files. Last but not least, you can use BioViz Connect to make new files, such as new types of genome coverage graphs, and then view those files in IGB with a simple button click.
