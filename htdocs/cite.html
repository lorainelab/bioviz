Citing IGB and ProtAnnot

If you use IGB in your research, please cite:
Integrated Genome Browser: Visual analytics platform for genomics
by Nowlan H. Freese, David C. Norris, and Ann E. Loraine
Bioinformatics 2016 Jul 15;32(14):2089-95.
If you use ProtAnnot in your research, please cite:
ProtAnnot: an App for Integrated Genome Browser to display how alternative splicing and transcription affect proteins
by Tarun Mall, John Eckstein, David C. Norris, Hiral Vora, Nowlan H. Freese, and Ann E. Loraine
Bioinformatics. 2016 Aug 15;32(16):2499-501
Citing IGB and IGB Apps like ProtAnnot will help us raise more funding to continue supporting the software.

Thank you for your help!
